# India-First Sales Readiness

This is the commercial launch plan for selling the NexusHealth to clinics. It is written for founder-led sales and small-team execution, not enterprise hospital procurement.

## Positioning

Use:

> AI-assisted clinic workflow software for appointments, patient records, clinician-reviewed summaries, and screening documentation.

Do not use:

- AI doctor
- autonomous diagnosis
- replaces clinicians
- guaranteed diagnosis
- regulator-cleared medical device
- hospital compliance already completed

## First Customer Profile

Best first buyers:

- Solo and small specialist clinics.
- Clinics with 1-10 doctors.
- Clinics already using WhatsApp, paper, spreadsheets, or basic appointment tools.
- Clinics that need faster record review, appointment organization, and patient follow-up.
- Clinics willing to run a two-week pilot with synthetic/demo data first, then controlled real data.

Avoid initially:

- Large hospitals.
- Government procurement.
- Insurance workflows.
- Emergency care workflows.
- Autonomous triage centers.
- Any buyer that requires external certification before a pilot.

## Offer

Start with a controlled paid pilot:

- Duration: 14 days.
- Users: up to 3 clinicians and 3 staff.
- Data: synthetic demo data first; real patient data only after privacy notice and clinic approval.
- Scope: appointment workflow, profile management, health-record organization, clinician-reviewed summaries, report download, audit logs.
- Support: one onboarding session, one mid-pilot review, one closeout report.

Success metrics:

- Appointment booking time reduced.
- Health-record retrieval time reduced.
- Clinician acceptance rate for AI summaries.
- Number of reports generated.
- Number of audit-log entries captured.
- Support tickets by severity.
- Staff/doctor satisfaction score.

## Packaging

Suggested India pricing bands for early sales:

| Plan | Buyer | Monthly Price Target | Included |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Clinic Starter | 1 doctor | INR 2,999-4,999 | appointments, records, chat, reports, audit logs |
| Clinic Team | 2-5 doctors | INR 7,999-14,999 | Starter plus multi-doctor workflow and admin review |
| Clinic Group | 6-20 doctors | Custom | onboarding, deployment support, retention settings |

Discount rules:

- Offer a pilot credit only if the clinic converts within 30 days.
- Do not offer unlimited free pilots.
- Do not discount below hosting/support cost.
- Charge setup for any custom deployment.

## Sales Process

1. Discovery call
2. Demo using synthetic patients only
3. Trust-baseline review
4. Pilot order and data-processing terms
5. Controlled pilot
6. Closeout report
7. Convert to monthly subscription

Discovery questions:

- How are appointments booked today?
- Where are patient records stored?
- Who can access records?
- How often do doctors need to review old reports?
- What wastes the most staff time?
- Who approves software purchases?
- Are you using ABHA/ABDM workflows today?

## Pilot Entry Gate

Before handling real patient data:

- Clinic owner approves the pilot scope.
- Privacy notice is shared.
- Clinic data-processing terms are accepted.
- Admin user is assigned.
- Staff access roles are reviewed.
- Production secrets are configured.
- Audit logs are enabled and visible.
- Backups and deletion process are documented.

## India Compliance Posture

Baseline:

- DPDP-aligned privacy notice and consent flow.
- Clear purpose limitation.
- Data retention and deletion process.
- Grievance/contact point.
- Audit trail for sensitive actions.
- Clinician accountability for medical decisions.
- ABDM compatibility roadmap.

Telemedicine:

- Doctors remain accountable.
- The product supports workflow and documentation.
- The product must not prescribe or independently treat.

Medical-device risk:

- Avoid diagnosis/treatment claims in early sales.
- If the product is positioned as diagnosis or treatment software, perform a CDSCO medical-device software review before sale.
