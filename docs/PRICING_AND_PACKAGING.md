# Pricing And Packaging

These are starting points for India-first clinic sales. Adjust after real pilots, not before.

## Pricing Principles

- Charge for workflow value, not AI novelty.
- Keep the first plan affordable for solo clinics.
- Avoid unlimited free usage.
- Charge separately for custom deployment, integrations, migration, and training.
- Do not include regulated claims in plan names or marketing.

## India Launch Plans

| Plan | Target Buyer | Monthly Price Target | Users | Best For |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Clinic Starter | solo clinic | INR 2,999-4,999 | 1 doctor + 2 staff | appointments, records, reports |
| Clinic Team | small clinic | INR 7,999-14,999 | up to 5 doctors + 10 staff | multi-doctor workflows |
| Clinic Group | clinic group | custom | custom | onboarding, retention, deployment support |

## Feature Packaging

Clinic Starter:

- Appointments.
- Patient profiles.
- Health records.
- AI-assisted clinician summaries.
- Health report downloads.
- Basic audit log.

Clinic Team:

- Everything in Starter.
- Multiple doctors.
- Admin audit review.
- Doctor specialization workflow.
- Priority support.
- Pilot closeout report.

Clinic Group:

- Everything in Team.
- Deployment support.
- Data retention settings.
- Security review support.
- Integration discovery.
- Staff onboarding.

## Paid Pilot

Recommended:

- INR 9,999-24,999 for 14 days depending on clinic size.
- Credit pilot fee against first paid month only if the clinic converts within 30 days.
- Use synthetic data until paperwork and deployment gates are completed.

## Do Not Bundle Initially

- Full hospital integrations.
- Claims processing.
- Government procurement customization.
- Autonomous triage.
- Prescription automation.
- Unreviewed WhatsApp/SMS automation.
- Custom AI model training on clinic data.

## Commercial Terms To Decide

Before the first real customer:

- Billing cycle.
- Refund policy.
- Support hours.
- Data export format.
- Termination/deletion timeline.
- Uptime target.
- Incident contact.
- Subprocessor list.
- Data hosting region.
