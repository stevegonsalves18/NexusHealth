# Contract Packet Checklist

This checklist is for preparing clinic sales documents. It is not legal advice and is not a substitute for local counsel.

## India Pilot Packet

Minimum documents before real patient data:

- Order form or pilot agreement.
- Privacy notice.
- Clinic data-processing terms.
- Product scope and blocked claims.
- Support and incident contact.
- Subprocessor and hosting-region list.
- Data retention/deletion schedule.
- Backup and restore evidence owner.
- Incident response owner and escalation channel.
- Legal-hold and retention evidence owner.
- Security assurance evidence owner.
- Pilot success metrics.
- Security questionnaire.

## Order Form Fields

- Clinic legal name.
- Clinic address.
- Buyer/admin contact.
- Pilot dates.
- User count.
- Included workflows.
- Excluded workflows.
- Price and payment terms.
- Support contact.
- Data handling scope.
- Termination process.

## Data Processing Terms

Cover:

- Purpose of processing.
- Data categories.
- Authorized users.
- Confidentiality obligations.
- Security controls.
- Subprocessors.
- Breach/incident notice.
- Data return/deletion.
- Backup retention, restore, and deleted-data reintroduction controls.
- Incident response, breach notice, and alert escalation process.
- Legal-hold process and per-surface retention windows.
- Secret scan, dependency scan, SBOM, vulnerability scan, penetration-test evidence, and critical/high finding policy.
- Audit-log availability.
- Support access rules.

## Privacy Notice Fields

Cover:

- Who operates the product.
- What data is collected.
- Why data is collected.
- How data is used.
- Whether data is shared.
- Retention period.
- User rights and request process.
- Grievance/contact point.
- Safety disclaimer for AI outputs.

## Blocked Contract Language

Do not promise:

- Autonomous diagnosis.
- Treatment decisions without clinician review.
- Emergency response.
- Regulatory clearance not actually obtained.
- External security certifications not actually completed.
- Guaranteed clinical outcomes.
- Unlimited liability.
- Custom integrations without paid scope.

## Expansion Addenda

Europe:

- GDPR data processing agreement.
- Data subject rights workflow.
- Cross-border transfer review.
- AI Act / medical-device intended-use review.

United States:

- Business associate agreement readiness.
- HIPAA safeguard mapping.
- Breach notification process.
- FDA clinical decision support intended-use review.

Other countries:

- Local health-data privacy review.
- Local telemedicine rules.
- Data residency review.
- Medical-software classification.
